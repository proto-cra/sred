Apply for pre-claim approval

Learn how to apply for pre-claim approval before you begin your SR&ED project.

Requirements for pre-claim approval

Your planned work must meet all the eligibility criteria for an SR&ED tax incentive. In addition, you must meet all the following requirements for pre-claim approval.

Your business must:

  • Be either a Canadian-controlled private corporation or other Canadian corporation, or a partnership
  • Have a gross business income of less than $25M

Your project must:

  • Not have already been claimed in a previous tax year
  • Not involve issues that may be currently under litigation

How to apply for pre-claim approval

Follow the steps below to apply for pre-claim approval for an SR&ED tax incentive.

  1. Ask for a case number

    Confirm you meet the requirements, get the case number you need to submit your application

  2. Complete your application

    Answer questions about your project, gather your supporting documents

  3. Submit your application

    Upload your application in My Business Account in your CRA account

  4. After you apply

    Meet with a Research and Technology Advisor, receive our decision
