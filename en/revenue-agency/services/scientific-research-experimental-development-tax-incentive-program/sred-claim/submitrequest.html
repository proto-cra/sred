Fill out the Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SR&ED) pre-claim approval application form to describe your planned project(s).

We recommend your (senior) personnel, who are familiar with the scientific or technological content of the work, are involved in preparing the application. Involving staff who have in-depth knowledge of the proposed SR&ED work, will help ensure the accuracy of the information you provide.

Part 1 - General information

Line 010 – Name of claimant
Enter the registered business name. If the claim is filed for a partnership, enter the name of the partnership.
Line 012 Business identification
Enter the identification number that pertains to your business. If you are a partnership and have a business number (BN), enter the business number. Make sure that you enter all 15 characters of the BN.
Line 014 – Number of projects
Enter the total number of projects you are requesting pre-claim approval for (maximum 3).
Line 016 – Pre-claim approval reference number
Enter the reference number provided by the CRA to apply for pre-claim approval.

Part 2 - Project information

You must complete this part for each project of your pre-claim approval request.

Purpose

On a project-by-project basis, provide information that establishes the nature of your planned scientific research and experimental development (SR&ED) work. This information enables the CRA to carry out an initial review of the planned work. This review helps to establish how the planned work meets the SR&ED eligibility requirements and may allow the CRA to process your application faster.

We recommend that personnel who are familiar with the scientific or technical content of the planned SR&ED project(s) prepare this part of the application form.

SR&ED project definitions

An SR&ED project must fall within the definition of SR&ED contained in the Income Tax Act. An SR&ED project comprises a set of interrelated activities that collectively are necessary to resolve specific scientific or technological uncertainties in the attempt to achieve the scientific or technological advancements defined for the project. This advancement is pursued through a systematic investigation or search in a field of science or technology by means of experiment or analysis performed by qualified individuals.

When you frame your descriptions of the planned work, we recommend that you follow the above characteristics in defining your SR&ED project.

References: Income Tax Act subsection 248(1); Eligibility of Work for SR&ED Investment Tax Credits Policy

Provide clear and concise information

This is your first opportunity to present the planned work in the context of the SR&ED program. Clear and concise descriptions of the planned work that directly addresseses these questions will enable the CRA to expedite your application.

There is a word limit for each question in Section B. Therefore, your answers should concentrate on the technical facts and should be in the technical language and style of those who understand and are familiar with the planned work. You may include more details in the supporting documents, such as your project plan.

Section A – Project identification

Provide information on the nature and scope of the planned SR&ED work.

Line 020 – Project title
Enter the title of the project. If you have a title and an identification code for the project, enter both.
Line 022 – Duration of the pre-claim approval period requested
Select the duration of the pre-claim approval period requested for the project (1, 2 or 3 years).
Line 024 – ??
Enter the total estimated SR&ED expenditures.
Line 026 – Anticipated SR&ED project start date
Enter the start date of the SR&ED project. The start of the project is defined as the point at which planned work commences to resolve the identified scientific or technological uncertainty.
Line 028 – Anticipated SR&ED project completion date
Enter the expected completion date of the SR&ED project. The completion date of the project is defined as the point at which the planned work is expected to resolve the identified uncertainty, or you plan to terminate the project for any reason.
Line 030 – Field of science or technology code
From the table in Appendix 1 of the T4088 guide, select the field of science or technology that best describes the primary field of your SR&ED project. This may not necessarily be the same as the field of science or technology of the company project or in which the company carries out its regular business.
The field of science and technology you enter on this line is used for statistical and resource management purposes only and not for the purpose of determining eligibility of the work. Appendix 1 is not an exhaustive list of all eligible fields of science or technology.

Lines 034 to 036
Provide the name, position title and phone number of the person best suited to provide the supporting technical information for the planned project. This person, or another individual from the business with this knowledge, will be required to attend the meeting with the CRA.
Line 038 to 040 – Project pre-claim history
Indicate if you have previously made a pre-claim approval request for this project. Select only one. If a project is a continuation from a predecessor company, and it is the first time you are making a pre-claim approval request, select 038 (first pre-claim approval request for this project).
