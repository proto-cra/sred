Review your application

Claimants are responsible for the information in their application. Whether you prepare the application yourself or it is prepared by someone else, check that your application is accurate and complete before you submit it.

A complete application must include a completed pre-claim approval application form and the required supporting documentation.

Include all your documents

Upload all your documents in the same session. If you donâ€™t, it is possible to continue uploading in a second session, but if you are too delayed your file may be closed for being incomplete.

If your session expires before you upload all your documents and select Submit, your progress will not be saved.

Submit your application online

Submit your completed pre-claim approval application form and all supporting documents online using your CRA account.

CRA sign in

  1. Sign in to your CRA account
  2. Select your Business account to access My Business Account
  3. On the Overview page, under "Correspondence," select Submit documents
  4. When asked if you have a case or reference number, select Yes
  5. Enter the case number you got from the CRA
  6. Upload up to 10 documents

For details about accepted formats and sizes, refer to: Submit documents online
