Ask for a reference number

Before you can submit your pre-claim approval application, you must get a reference number from the CRA.

When you submit the following web form, the CRA will review your eligibility and contact you to:

  • Give you a reference number and further instructions

  • Explain why you do not meet the requirements to apply for pre-claim approval
